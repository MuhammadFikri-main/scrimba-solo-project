Visit Johor

Enjoy culture and entertainment in this futuristic city in south of Malaysia

Top three activities to do in Johor

Clock Tower

Explore the historic Clock Tower in Johor, a symbol of the city's rich heritage. Marvel at its intricate architecture and learn about its significance to the local community.

Legoland Malaysia

Experience endless fun and adventure at Legoland Malaysia. From thrilling rides to impressive LEGO creations, there's something for everyone at this world-renowned theme park.

Sultan Ibrahim Stadium

Catch exciting sports events and concerts at Sultan Ibrahim Stadium. With its state-of-the-art facilities and vibrant atmosphere, it's a must-visit destination for sports enthusiasts and music lovers alike.

Your Guide

Meet Muhammad Fikri, your friendly local guide eager to show you the best of Johor. With his expertise and passion for hospitality, he ensures every visitor leaves with unforgettable memories and a smile.
